A Legacy of Fine Fragrance

Noor Candles & Co. was born from a passion for tradition and quality. The name, meaning "light," guides our promise to provide products that are pure, elegant, and ethically crafted.

We use only the finest natural waxes and custom-blended essential oils. Every candle is **hand-poured in small batches**, ensuring meticulous attention to detail and a flawless, long-lasting burn. We believe luxury should be responsible.
